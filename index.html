OUR BLOGS

ABUSE

Abuse is a harmful act where one person controls or harms another, causing emotional, physical, or psychological damage. Recognizing and addressing it is essential to protect and support victims, promoting dignity and safety for all.

ADDICTION

Addiction is a compulsive behavior where an individual becomes dependent on a substance or activity, leading to significant harm in their life. Recognizing and addressing addiction is crucial for recovery and restoring well-being and balance.

BIPOLAR DISORDER

Bipolar disorder is a mental health condition characterized by extreme mood swings, including episodes of mania and depression. Recognizing and managing the disorder is essential for maintaining stability and improving quality of life.
